User-level Sentiment Analysis

The information about social relationships can be used to improve user-level sentiment analysis. The main motivation behind our approach is that users that are somehow "connected" may be more likely to hold similar opinions; therefor, relationship informations can complement what we can extract about a user's viewpoints from their utterances.

Information Flow Detection

The real-time information on news sites, blogs and social networking sites changes dynamically and spreads rapidly through the Web. Developing methods for handling such information at a massive scale requires that we think about how information content varies over time, how it is transmitted, and how it mutates as it spreads.

Big Data on Scientific and Information Visualization

Unstructured Text Visualization

Text data is used as primary information artifact in current human society. The availability of text data has grown tremendously in recent years due to networking infrastructure. How to effective extract and represent the semantic information among text data via the suitable visual representation is critical to help users in understanding and gathering information from text.

Resume Information Visualization

Resume data as a special text data contains valuable semantic information regarding spatial-temporal development trajectory of person’s grades. In addition, the latent social relations between different person can be revealed by mining resume data. Extract & visualizing such semantic information and constructing the social network behind resume data can benefit many applications such as appoint-and-dismiss decision supporting and social mining.

Event Detection and Visualization

It is increasingly difficult for users to hold the full picture of massive events in a comprehensive perspective when facing large-scale online streams. In addition, how to support human on perceiving the important rare events, which may trigger the subsequent emergency events, remains to be a big challenge. We present a framework to detect, visualize and perceive the multi-grained events, which gives users a full picture of relevant events and supports high-quality decision making accordingly.

Structured Data Visualization

Structured data such as scalar field data can be visualized as a contour map to indicate the latent physical structure and trend behind data itself. Human can gain valuable insights from such contour map accordingly. However, dealing with increasing amount of data attributes or dimensions arises a challenge: how to visualize the data with multi-dimensions in a cohesive view and explore the relations between the data on various dimensions. To address this challenge, we stand on the perspective of multi-variable data fusion and propose a overlap-based contouring method.
