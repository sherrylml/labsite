Big Data on Social Media Mining and Analytics

Social Tie Analysis

Interpersonal ties are responsible for the structure of social networks and the transmission of information through these networks. Different types of social ties have essentially different influence on people. Awareness fo the types of social ties can benefit many applictions such as recommendation and community detection.

User-level Sentiment Analysis

Information about social relationships can be used to improve user-level sentiment analysis. The main motivation behind our approach is that users that are somehow "connected" may be more likely to hold similar opinions; therefor, relationship informations can complement what we can extract about a user's viewpoints from their utterances.

Information Flow Detection

The real-time information on news sites, blogs and social networking sites changes dynamically and spreads rapidly through the Web. Developing methods for handling such information at a massive scale requires that we think about how information content varies over time, how it is transmitted, and how it mutates as it spreads.

Big Data on Scientific and Information Visualization

Unstructured Text Visualization

Text data is used as primary information artifact in current human society. The availability of text data has grown tremendously in recent years due to networking infrastructure. How to effective extract and represent the semantic information among text data via the suitable visual representation is critical to help users in understanding and gathering information from text.

Event Detection and Visualization

Recently, Event Detection attracts much attention as it can help the decision makers to efficiently detect attractive events. However, it is increasingly difficult for users to hold the full picture of massive events in a comprehensive perspective when facing large-scale online streams. In addition, how to support human on perceiving the important rare events, which may trigger the subsequent emergency events, remains to be a big challenge. We present a framework to detect, visualize and perceive the multi-grained events, which gives users a full picture of relevant events and supports high-quality decision making accordingly

Structured Data Visualization

Structured data such as scalar field data can be visualized as a contour map to indicate the latent physical structure and trend behind data itself. Human can gain valuable insights from such contour map accordingly, such as the underground geologic structure and the distribution of mineral resources. However, dealing with increasing amount of data attributes or dimensions arises a challenge: how to visualize the data with multi-dimensions in a cohesive view and explore the relations between the data on various dimensions. To address this challenge, we stand on the perspective of multi-variable data fusion and propose a overlap-based contouring method.
